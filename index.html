Seiko SKX

  • I created this image for my favourite watch, the Seiko SKX009. The watch itself was in production from 1996 to 2019, which for a watch model is a very long time. Even though it was first introduced in the 90s, the design of the watch goes back to the late 70s and early 80s, which is why I went with the retro/vaporwave aesthetic.

DZ60

  • I created this work for my favourite keyboard. It is a custom built 60% board with SA profile keycaps on cherry mx brown switches in a 3D printed case. Mechanical keyboards are a somewhat omnipresent hobby of mine, but I am not as passionate about keyboards as I am about watch collecting. This was my attempt at making something in a retro advertisement artstyle.

CASIO CALCULATOR

  • This was my first attempt at making something with an 80s aesthetic. I made it in photoshop rather than illustrator as I did not have illustrator on my home pc at the time. It is a casio calculator watch in the style of the original 1989 Gameboy box art.

Gun Sign

  • This is part of a series of assets I made for a 3D class assignment, they were to be used as image assets for background use. It is designed in the style of the blade runner pistol. All signs in this series were made to look as if they existed in a cyberpunk city, ei 2019 L.A. in Blade Runner

Noodle bar neon

  • This was another asset created for the assignment mentioned in the previous image. It is a sign for a fictional noodle bar. All of the kanji text used in the series is just the Japanese equivalent of what the sign says in English.

Sake Neon

  • This is another image that is a part of the neon sign series. It is a sign for a fictional sake bar, the main part of the sign with the vertical lines is based off of one of the neon signs seen in blade runner, as well as the script font, which is also based off of a different sign seen in the movie.

Seiko Neon

  • This is another image that is a part of the neon sign series. It is a fictional representation of what I think a Seiko neon sign would look like in the Blade Runner universe. The kanji text just says seiko. the 1881 in the clock is the year Seiko was founded. The clock shows the time as around 10:10, which is the time that all product shots for watches shows as it best frames the watch face and its features.

Augments Sign

  • This sign is also from the neon sign series. It is designed for a fictional human augmentation store, like the kind seen in the game Deus Ex. The face is based off of the box art for system shock 2. The backwards R is a reference to the store toys r us.
